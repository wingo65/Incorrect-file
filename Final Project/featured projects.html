Movie Project

This project constituted a pivotal assignment during the second term of my pursuit of an associate's degree. The task at hand was the development of a sophisticated movie seating chart application, meticulously designed to simulate the experience of users engaged in the process of purchasing tickets for a cinematic experience. The application seamlessly facilitated users in visualizing the seating chart, distinguishing between available and occupied seats, with marked indicators for the latter. Prospective customers were afforded the ability to seamlessly proceed with ticket acquisition. This involved specifying the quantity of tickets desired, selecting preferred seating arrangements, and receiving an instant computation of the total cost. Notably, the application provided a comprehensive feature enabling users to review their entire transaction history. This encompassed a detailed breakdown of the total number of tickets purchased and the corresponding cumulative expenditure. The incorporation of such functionalities aimed to enhance user experience, providing a seamless and professional ticketing solution for cinema enthusiasts.
